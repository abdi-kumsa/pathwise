What is this project?

You are building a dual-module AI-powered analytics platform — not just an "AI agent," but a full-stack data product that combines machine learning models, a data pipeline, and interactive dashboards. The two modules work together: one forecasts market/employer demand, and the other uses that intelligence to guide student enrollment decisions.

🤖
Is it an AI Agent?

Partially. The recommender engine acts like an agent (takes inputs → reasons → recommends). But most of the system is ML models + dashboards.

🧠
Is it an LLM?

No. It uses classical ML (LSTM, XGBoost, Prophet) for forecasting, not a large language model — though you could optionally add one for explanations.

📈
What is it really?

A Predictive Analytics Dashboard — data pipeline → ML models → visualizations → recommendation engine. Think Power BI but custom-built with AI.

🎯
Who uses it?

University administrators (enrollment strategy), department heads (curriculum reform), and students (placement readiness evaluator).

Module 1 – Market Demand Forecast Dashboard
01
Market Demand & Hiring Analytics
For institutions to understand where and how graduates are being hired

📊 Key Dashboard Components

  • Top Hiring Companies & Designations — Bar charts + tree maps showing highest-volume recruiters and most in-demand job titles
  • Regional Hiring Trends — Geographic maps of job/internship availability by city or region ("vibrant regions")
  • Hiring Source Analysis — Bar graphs of most active recruitment channels (LinkedIn, portals, etc.)
  • Job vs. Internship Ratios — Pie charts showing full-time vs. experiential learning balance
  • Skill Demand vs. Training Availability — Maps industry needs (AI, Big Data) against current curricula
  • Skill-to-Market Fit — Classifies graduates as Matched / Overeducated / Undereducated
  • Placement Probability Evaluator — Student inputs GPA, internships, projects → receives real-time placement score
  • Salary Forecasting — Predicts early-career earnings by major and market demand
  • Feature Importance Charts — Shows which factors (CGPA, internships, communication) most impact employability
  • Escape Route Mapping — Pathways for underemployed graduates to move into better-aligned jobs
02
Student Enrollment Dashboard
Aligns institutional intake strategy with real labor market demand

📊 Key Dashboard Components

  • Enrollment Trend Forecasts (LSTM) — Predicts future enrollment based on historical demographic data
  • Admission Risk Preference — Sliders for admins to model over/under-enrollment risk at 5%, 10%, 20% thresholds
  • Placement Rate by Stream — Compares departments (CS, Electronics, Civil) by market alignment
  • Interactive Recommender (Student Portal) — Students input CGPA, IQ, internships, soft skills → get placement likelihood score + skill-building roadmap
  • Course & Certification Recommendations — AI engine suggests SAP, SPSS, AI certifications to bridge skill gaps
  • KPI Cards — Total companies hiring, placement rate by stream, average time to find work

💡 Best Approach Recommendation

For a solo developer or small team, here's the fastest and most deliverable-focused route:

Use Mock/Sample Data FirstSimulate enrollment + job data in CSV. Don't wait for real data to start building.
FastAPI + React StackFaster than Flask for APIs. Pair with Recharts for rich, interactive charts in one codebase.
Start with XGBoostBuild placement predictor first — it's the most impressive feature and easiest to demo.
Prophet for ForecastingEasiest time-series model to get running quickly. Switch to LSTM only if you need more accuracy.
